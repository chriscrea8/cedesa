About Us

Centre for Education Development and Skills Acquisition (CEDESA) is a non-governmental organization with the mission of bringing sanity to education. It is also specially committed to assist youths especially the indigent ones in rural and semi-urban areas to achieve and attain destinies through transformational education.

TAMING AND TERMINATING EXAMINATION MALPRACTICE IN NIGERIAN SCHOOLS

Education in Nigeria has consistently been under a siege which makes it difficult to come out of declension, degeneration and devaluation. Over the years, many factors have been responsible for these negative education conditions. Ranging from parental wrong training, students’ lack of focus, teachers’ lack of dedication, governments unconcern attitude to the state of education, incompetency of school administrators and inadequate number of teachers in core subjects, just to mention a few. All these factors have led to one problem or the other especially the major one called examination malpractices which will be discussed in this write-up. She is committed to helping youths in fulfilling destinies and reducing crime in schools.

Our Mission

To establish educational excellence amongst youths, by educating parents on the evil and danger of encouraging their wards in engaging in examination malpractices, and increasing awareness on the need for quality education via various means.

Also, to inspire educators involved, to be diligent and consistent in educating the youths, thereby assisting them in reaching their potentials, so as to contribute positively to the nation's economy and well-being.

Training and Rules

Every CEDESA youth must comply to the following rules and regulations during training sessions

DURING TRAINING SESSIONS

Every CEDESA youths must be prompt with time, lateness to trainings and mentorship session is highly prohibited

Every CEDESA is expected to behave in an orderly manner while the training and mentorship session is ongoing, side talks, pressing of mobile device is prohibited

Every CEDESA youth is expected to dress decently. Indecent clothes revealing sensitive parts of the body is highly prohibited

AT THE COMMUNITY

Every CEDESA youth is expected to demonstrate a good behaviour outside the walls of the CEDESA family

It is a crime and abomination for a CEDESA youths to engage in outright fight or outburst anger to the tone of gathering crowd and uproar

Any CEDESA youths taking hard drugs such as tramadol, cocaine, marijuana, indian hemp and other related drugs with. Alcoholic drinks and cigarette shall be expelled from the CEDESA club

Any CEDESA youths living in immorality as a way of life shall be expelled from the CEDESA club

AT SCHOOL

Every CEDESA youth is expected to exempt himself/herself from all kind of examination malpractices, misconduct, unruly behavior and attitude toward the school authority and the teachers

Every CEDESA youth is expected to be academically sound in order to enhance the image of the organization

Every CEDESA youth is expected to make use of correct and simple English as medium of communication at school and other public places. Pidgin English is highly prohibited

AT HOME

Every CEDESA youth is expected to demonstrate a good behaviour at home aiming at influencing his/her siblings positively

Every CEDESA youth is expected to make the organization proud everywhere he or she finds himself / herself
